My Projects

HeadHigh | Graphic Design
for Independent Bands and Labels

My obsession with music borders on the manic side. It has always been a goal of mine to help unsigned and independent artists make their way in the music industry.

HeadHigh provides an outlet for me to offer affordable graphic design to the thousands of talented unsigned artists and independent labels trying to stand out from the crowd.

Headhigh also allows me to flex my design muscles in ways that I may not have always been able to at previous employers. With a focus mainly on branding & identity design, print and illustration, my design skills have and will certainly improve.

VISIT SITE

How Long
'Til Trumps Gone?

My first personal digital project resulted in a countdown to the end of Donald Trump’s Presidency.

His divisive campaign for election (and his sexist behaviour) inspired me to create something in opposition of his Presidency. The countdown receives thousands of views a month, and has been described as both ‘a reverse doomsday clock’ and an ‘attack on the greatest living President’.

This personal project also provided great experience with social media, and displayed how effective a strong social media presence can be. Utilising scheduled tweets, original content and interaction with followers, I grew the account to over 1000 followers in just a few short months.
